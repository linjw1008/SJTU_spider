Agenda:

















Cher Whee Sim is a senior HR Leader, with
25+ years of experience working in the semiconductor industry. She has shaped
talent into high performers at Micron using progressive technical knowledge,
engaging diverse communication methods and employing innovative human resource
programs to attract and retain talent. She has successfully spearheaded
projects by balancing the human element and corporate objectives resulting in
empowered teams with a high desire to pursue organizational excellence in the
fast-growing semiconductor industry. She has spent over 13 years leading
regionally and culturally diverse HR teams specifically in the Talent
Acquisition arena.  Cher Whee has
architected strategies to attracting top talent who exemplify Micron’s core
values and enable Micron’s vision and mission.


沈芝慧女士是一位从事在半导体行业25年的资深人力资源领袖。她利用先进的技术知识和多元化的沟通方法以及采用创新的人力资源项目去吸引和保留人才，使其成为高绩效的美光人才。她通过平衡人力因素和公司的目标，成功的领导能够使团队具有在快速发展的半导体行业追求卓越的强烈愿望的项目。她花了13年时间领导区域和文化多元化的人力资源团队，特别是在人才招揽领域。沈芝慧女士制定了能够吸引体现美光的核心价值观，实现了美光愿景和使命的顶尖人才的战略。
