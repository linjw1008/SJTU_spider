title: 关于留学生开具无犯罪记录证明的通知
date: [ 2019年5月23日 ]
text: 

根据通知，目前留学生开具“无犯罪记录证明”流程有所更新，原先的无犯罪记录证明电子版及纸质版将不再适用。


请后续需要开具该证明的同学直接前往电信群楼3-109满艺老师处。





According to the circular, the current procedure for applying the "Certificate of No Criminal Record" has been updated, and the original version of electronic and paper versions will no longer be applicable. Please go directly to Room 109, Telecom Group Building 3 to apply for the certificate.
