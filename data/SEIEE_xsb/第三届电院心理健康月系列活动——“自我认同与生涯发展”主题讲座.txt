活动内容：


  Dr. Veronica Orozco, Licensed Psychologist in University Health Services, UC Berkeley, provide individual, couples, group and crisis counseling to undergraduate and graduate students. Interests include resilience of Latina/o students and other first-generation, low-income, underrepresented students, undocumented students, adjustment issues, anxiety, depression, loss/grief, relationship concerns, family of origin issues, LGBTQ ally. She also provide career counseling including interpretation of career assessments (Strong Interest Inventory, Skills Confidence Inventory, and Myers-Briggs Type Inventory). And today she will give lecture on Self identity and career development.

主讲人：Dr. Veronica Orozco









   Veronica Orozco教授现在在加州大学伯克利分校从事大学健康服务，主要包括咨询和心理服务。她博士毕业于俄亥俄州立大学，临床经验丰富，曾在密歇根大学，加利福尼亚州立大学，俄亥俄州立大学都从事了有关的心理工作，为大学辅导中心的本科生和研究生提供咨询服务。对学生多样化种族/阶级，性倾向的问题提供指导。她擅长解决各种心理问题，包括抑郁症，焦虑症，人际关系问题和婚姻问题，为博士生实习生，博士后研究员和职业实习生提供培训和监督。对心理问题有着很深刻的理解，也有着十分专业的知识储备。
